Active Calculus - Multivariable

Active Calculus - Multivariable supports an active learning approach to a course involving multivariable and vector calculus. Every section of Active Calculus - Multivariable offers preview activities designed around preparing users for new topics; multiple activities in each section that can be used either in- or out-of-classtime; interactive WeBWorK exercises; other exercises and tasks that foster students' practice of skills and deeper investigation of conceptual material; opportunities for students to refine their effective communication of geometric and algebraic perspectives.
