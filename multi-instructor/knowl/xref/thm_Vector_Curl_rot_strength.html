Theorem 13.7.18.

The rotational strength of a vector field \(\vF\) around an axis given by a unit vector \(\vv\) at a point \(P=(a,b,c)\) can be computed by \(\left(\curl(\vF)(a,b,c)\right)\cdot \vv\text{.}\)
