Solution 9.4.7.5.1.

\(\vec{OP}=\left\lt 0,7,4\right>\) and \(\vec{OQ}=\left\lt 0,5,6\right>\text{.}\) Thus, \(S=\frac{1}{2} || \vec{OP} \times \vec{OQ} ||\) We compute the cross product: \(\vec{OP} \times \vec{OQ}= \left| \begin{array}{ccc} \boldsymbol{i} \amp \boldsymbol{j} \amp \boldsymbol{k} \\ 0 \amp 7 \amp 4 \\ 0 \amp 5 \amp 6 \end{array} \right| = \left| \begin{array}{cc} 7 \amp 4 \\ 5 \amp 6 \end{array} \right| \boldsymbol{i} - \left| \begin{array}{cc} 0 \amp 4 \\ 0 \amp 6 \end{array} \right| \boldsymbol{j} + \left| \begin{array}{cc} 0 \amp 7 \\ 0 \amp 5 \end{array} \right| \boldsymbol{k}\) \(\qquad = 22 \boldsymbol{i} + 0 \boldsymbol{j} + 0 \boldsymbol{k}\) \(S=\frac{1}{2}|| 22 \boldsymbol{i} + 0 \boldsymbol{j} + 0 \boldsymbol{k} || =\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{(22)^2+(0)^2+(0)^2} \approx 11.\)
