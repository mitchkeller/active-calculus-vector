Solution 12.8.5.2.1.

(a) Since \(\vec F = 10 x e^{y}\,\vec i + 5 x^2 e^{y}\,\vec j\) is a gradient vector field and \(C\) is a closed curve, \(\int_C \vec F\cdot d\vec r = 0\text{.}\) (b) Since
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(5(x + y)) - \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(10(x - y)) = 15, \end{equation*}
by Green's Theorem,
\begin{equation*} \int_C\vec G\cdot d\vec r = \int_R\left(15\right)\,dA = 15(\mbox{Area of triangle}) = 120. \end{equation*}
