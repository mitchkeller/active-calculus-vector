(a) The displacement from \(P\) to \(Q\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \overrightarrow{PQ} = (16\,\vec i + 9\,\vec j) - (1\,\vec i + 1\,\vec j) = 15\,\mathit{\vec i}+8\,\mathit{\vec j}. \end{equation*}
Since
\begin{equation*} \mbox{len}{\overrightarrow{PQ}} = \sqrt{15^2 + 8^2} = 17, \end{equation*}
a unit vector \(\vec u\) in the direction of \(\overrightarrow{PQ}\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \vec u = \frac{1}{17}\,\overrightarrow{PQ} = \frac{1}{17}\,(15\,\mathit{\vec i}+8\,\mathit{\vec j}) . \end{equation*}
(b) A vector of length 34 pointing in the same direction is given by
\begin{equation*} 34\,\vec u = \frac{34}{17}\,(15\,\mathit{\vec i}+8\,\mathit{\vec j}) = 30\,\mathit{\vec i}+16\,\mathit{\vec j}. \end{equation*}
