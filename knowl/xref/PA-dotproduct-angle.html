part 9.3.1.c.

When we look at vectors drawn with the same initial point, as you have done with the vectors in Figure 9.3.1, we can consider the angle between two vectors to be the smaller angle formed by looking at them. This results in an angle between \(0\) and \(\pi\) if we measure in radians or between \(0^{\circ}\) and \(180^{\circ}\) if we measure in degrees.
