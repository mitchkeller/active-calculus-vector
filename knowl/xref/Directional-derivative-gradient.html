Key Idea 11.7.13. The directional derivative and the gradient.

Given a differentiable function \(f = f(x,y)\) and a unit vector \(\vu = \langle u_1, u_2 \rangle\text{,}\) we may compute \(D_{\vu}f(x,y)\) by
\begin{equation} D_{\vu}f(x,y) = \nabla f(x,y) \cdot \vu.\tag{11.7.5} \end{equation}
