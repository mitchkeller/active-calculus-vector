The given vector has length \(\left| \langle -6, 3, 6 \rangle \right| = \sqrt{(-6)^2+3^2+6^2}= \sqrt{81}\text{,}\) so a unit vector in the direction of \(\langle -6, 3, 6 \rangle\) is \(\mathbf u = \frac{1}{\sqrt{81}}\langle -6, 3, 6 \rangle\text{.}\)
A vector in the same direction but with length \(3\) is
\(\displaystyle 3 \mathbf u = \frac{3}{\sqrt{81}} \langle -6, 3, 6 \rangle = \left \langle \frac{-18}{\sqrt{81}} , \frac{9}{\sqrt{81}}, \frac{18}{\sqrt{81}} \right \rangle\text{.}\)
