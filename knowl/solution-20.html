Solution 9.5.5.9.1.

(a) The time in minutes to go 10 miles at a speed of \(s\) mph is \((10/s)(60)= 600/s\text{.}\) Thus the 160 lb person going 11 mph uses \((10.8)(600/11) = 589.091\) calories, and the 180 lb person going 10 mph uses \((10.2)(600/10) = 612\) calories. (b) The 160 lb person burns \(589.091/160 = 3.68182\) calories per pound for the trip, while the 180 lb person burns \(612/180 = 3.4\) calories per pound for the trip.
