Definition 9.3.10.

Let \(\vu\) and \(\vv\) be vectors in \(\R^n\text{.}\) The projection of \(\vu\) onto \(\vv\) is the vector
\begin{align} \proj_{\vv} \vu \amp = \frac{\vu \cdot \vv}{\vv\cdot\vv} \vv\tag{9.3.3}\\ \amp = \left(\frac{\vu \cdot \vv}{\vecmag{\vv}}\right) \frac{\vv}{\vecmag{\vv}}\text{.}\tag{9.3.4} \end{align}
