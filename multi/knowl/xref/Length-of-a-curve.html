Theorem 10.3.5. The length of a curve.

If \(\vr(t)\) parameterizes a curve \(C\) on an interval \([a,b]\) for which \(\vr\, '(t)\) exists at each point, then the length \(L\) of \(C\text{,}\) is given by
\begin{equation} L = \int_a^b \vecmag{\vr\, '(t)} \, dt.\tag{10.3.1} \end{equation}
