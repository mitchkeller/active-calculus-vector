Activity 10.3.3. Is it a property of the driver or the road?

In this activity, we want to determine if the different measurements we have described are a property of the driver or the road. A measurement is a property of the driver if the values of that measurement can be different for different drivers when measured at the same location on the racetrack. A measurement is a property of the road when different drivers must have the same values when measured at the same location on the racetrack.
